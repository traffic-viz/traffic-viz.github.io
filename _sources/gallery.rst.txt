Third-party examples
--------------------

Users in the community have been publishing some very appealing use cases on third-party websites. Here is an attempt to list some of them [1]_:

- `Thunderbolts and lightning, very very enlightening <https://spatialawareness.io/blog/thunderbolts-and-lightning/>`__
- `Flight Information Regions <https://observablehq.com/@xoolive/flight-information-regions>`__

.. [1] Please get in touch if you came across a nice use case not listed on this page.
